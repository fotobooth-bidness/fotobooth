FAQ

Good questions, answered

Everything you need to know before you book. Still curious? Reach out anytime.

How much space does the booth need?+
An open-air setup fits comfortably in about a 10×10 ft area with optional access to a standard power outlet. We can adapt to tighter spaces — just let us know your venue!
How far in advance should I book?+
Popular dates, especially weekends in spring and fall, book out weeks ahead. We recommend reserving as soon as you have a date. Last-minute requests are welcome when we have availability.
Do you travel to my venue?+
Yes. Local travel is included, and longer distances are available as an extended travel add-on with a custom quote. Reach out with your location and we'll give you an estimate.
What's included in setup and teardown?+
Setup and breakdown are included in every package. We arrive early to set up before your event starts and pack down afterward — you don't lift a finger.
Can the photo strips be branded for my event?+
Yes. Every package includes one custom photo strip design with your names, event date, or theme, plus a personalized welcome screen. Need more than one design? That's an easy add-on.
When do I get my photos?+
Other than the physical prints you get, guests share instantly at the event via AirDrop and QR code. Your full online gallery of every photo is delivered within 24–48 hours after the event.
What's your deposit and cancellation policy?+
A deposit reserves your date, with the balance due the day before the event. Full details are on your booking agreement — reach out and we'll walk you through it.
